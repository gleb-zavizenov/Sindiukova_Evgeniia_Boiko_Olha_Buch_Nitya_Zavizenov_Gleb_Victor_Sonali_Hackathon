Welcome to Ontario

The breathtaking views of Ontario's vast wilderness, endless forests and abundant wildlife awaits. Ontario is large and green province on the South of Canada. There are 250,000 lakes, including the Great Lakes bordering the US

The most popular cities in Ontarian region are Toronto or Ottawa. Funsters converge on Toronto's vibrant multicultural neighborhoods, where immigrants from far and wide live in “maple” harmony.

Whether you want to reconnect with nature or lose yourself in the excitement of the most multiculturally diverse and socially cohesive region on Earth. Ontario surprise you with the beauty of her scenery and welcome you with the warmth of her people

Toronto Downtown

Tronto’s most prominent attraction is the CN Tower, the tallest free-standing structure in the western hemisphere and it now includes EdgeWalk, a thrilling hands-free walk around the outside ledge of the tower.
